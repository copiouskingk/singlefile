Why We're Building This

Why 400+ protocols?

We've identified and built protocols for every AI governance scenario we've encountered. Each protocol handles a specific risk - from P-001 (basic profanity) to P-400 (complex regulatory compliance). This isn't overkill; it's comprehensive coverage.

How do 400 protocols work together?

Our Protocol Nexus (P-0) orchestrates all 400 protocols intelligently. Not every output needs every protocol - the system dynamically applies relevant protocols based on context, keeping latency under 100ms.

Isn't this overkill for most organizations?

You can start with our core protocol sets and scale up. But when you need protocol P-389 (healthcare privacy) or P-247 (financial compliance), it's already there, tested and ready. Better to have it and not need it.

Which AI models do you support?

We're starting with OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google models. Our goal is to support all major AI platforms. If you need a specific model, let us know and we'll prioritize it.

What about latency?

Our protocol system adds processing time to AI responses. We're working to optimize performance, but comprehensive governance takes time. Current tests show variable latency depending on which protocols are triggered.

Can I try it?

We're in early deployment phase. The system works but we're still optimizing performance and reliability. Join our early access program to help us refine the platform for real-world use.
